Hack-O-Uplift

Are you interested in uplifting yourself by working on a project ? Do you have thought or idea that could possibly bring a change or help people in any ways? HACK-O-UPLIFT is the first-ever community hackathon of the uplift project. Its an open hackathon , whether you are a participant at uplift or not, you just need to have an idea and a will to work on it. Hack-O-uplift will provide you opportunity where you can invest your 36 hrs in working with your team to plan, build, technique it & bring your project to life.

Register yourself and your team and get onboard.
