Hack-O-Uplift

Are you interested in uplifting yourself by working on a project? Do you have thought or idea that could possibly bring a change or help people in any ways? Hack-O-Uplift is the first-ever community hackathon of The Uplift Project. Its an open hackathon, whether you are a participant at uplift or not, you just need to have an idea and a will to work on it. Hack-O-Uplift will provide you opportunity where you can invest your 36 Hrs in working with your team to plan, build, technique it & bring your project to life.

Register yourself and your team and get onboard.

NOTE: You have to be in a team of minimum 2 & maximum 4 members.

Frequently Asked Questions

WHAT IS A HACKATHON?

A hackathon is an event, usually hosted by a tech company or organization, where programmers get together for a short period of time to collaborate on a project. The participants work rapidly and often work without sleep to achieve their task, as the events generally only last 24 hours or take place over a weekend.

HOW CAN WE REGISTER IN HACK-O-UPLIFT ?

You can register in Hack-O-Uplift via Devfolio. Simply click on the link "Apply with Devfolio'' provided on our website.

I AM A BEGINNER. AM I ELIGIBLE TO PARTICIPATE?

Yes, this is open to all people and if you are a beginner it's the best way to start your hackathon journey and we would love to have your participation.

WHAT IS THE SIZE OF TEAM?

It can be a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 4 members in a team. You cannot participate individually.

IS IT FREE OF COST?

Yes, Hack-O-Uplift is absolutely free of cost to participate.

WHO CAN PARTICIPATE?

Everyone can participate! We encourage anyone who is interested in learning more about Hackathons, programming, and the tech industry to join Hack-O-Uplift!

WHAT ARE THE PRIZES FOR WINNERS?

Prizes will be declared soon. Stay updated via social media platforms.

WHERE WILL IT BE HOSTED?

Hack-O-Uplift will be hosted in the month of September 2021, completely online.
